How it works

Three steps, no account required.

1

Click the heart

Use the heart icon that's already on every IKEA product card and product page — nothing new to learn.

2

Pick a room

A small picker opens right there on the page. Choose a room and you're done — no redirect, no login.

3

Track and export

Totals update live as you shop. When you're ready, export a print-ready PDF or email the whole list in one click.
